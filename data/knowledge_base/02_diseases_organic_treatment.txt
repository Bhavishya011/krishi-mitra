DISEASE 1: TAMATAR MEIN EARLY BLIGHT (Aageti Jhulsa Rog)
Scientific Name: Alternaria solani

PEHCHAAN (Symptoms — kaise pehchanein):
- Patto par chhote, gehere bhoore/kaale daag (dark brown/black spots on leaves)
- Daagon ke gird peeli ring hoti hai (yellow halo around spots) — "target board" jaisa pattern
- Daag dheere dheere bade hote hain aur patti jhaad jaati hai
- Neeche ki pattiyaan pehle prabhavit hoti hain (lower leaves first)
- Zyada garm aur nami mausam mein tezi se failta hai

Kab hota hai: Baarish ke baad, zyada nami mein (high humidity), ya jab temperature 24-30°C ho

URGENCY LEVEL: HIGH — 2-3 din mein ilaaj shuru karo, warna poora ped prabhavit ho sakta hai

ORGANIC ILAAJ (Organic Treatment):

1. NEEM OIL SPRAY (Sabse effective)
   Tarika: 
   - 4 chamach (4 tablespoon) pure neem oil
   - 1 chamach (1 tablespoon) liquid soap (emulsifier ke liye)
   - 1 litre paani
   Mix karo aur shyam ko (evening) spray karo — seedha patto par, neeche ki taraf bhi
   Kitni baar: Har 7-10 din mein, jab tak symptoms dikhen
   
2. BAKING SODA SPRAY
   Tarika:
   - 1 chamach (1 tsp) baking soda
   - 1 litre gaaram paani
   - 2-3 boond liquid soap
   Mix karke spray karo
   Kaam: Patto ki surface ka pH badha deta hai, fungus nahi pal sakta
   
3. NEEM PATTI KADHA (Neem Leaf Extract)
   Tarika:
   - 5 kg fresh neem patte
   - 20 litre paani mein boil karo (2 ghante)
   - Thanda karke chhan lo
   - 10 guna paani mila kar dilute karo
   - Spray karo

4. JEEVAMRIT SPRAY
   - 10% Jeevamrit + 90% paani ka ghol
   - Weekly spray karo — plant ki immunity badhata hai

BACHAO KE UPAY (Prevention):
- Fasal chakkar (Crop rotation) apnao — tomato ek hi jagah baar baar mat lagao
- Patto ke beech hawa aane do (proper spacing)
- Mulching karo — mitti ke chhinte patto par na padein
- Rog wale patte turant todo aur jalao — khet mein mat chodho

DISEASE 2: TAMATAR MEIN LATE BLIGHT (Picheti Jhulsa Rog)
Scientific Name: Phytophthora infestans

PEHCHAAN (Symptoms):
- Patto par bade, gehre haare/bhoore daag — jaise paani jala ho (water-soaked lesions)
- Patte ka neeche wala hissa — safed/grey roonh jaisi growth (white fluffy growth)
- Bahut tezi se failta hai — ek hafte mein poori fasal barbad ho sakti hai
- Tane par bhi bhoore/kaale daag

Kab hota hai: Thandi raat + theek-theek nami + din mein moderate temperature

URGENCY LEVEL: VERY HIGH — aaj hi ilaaj shuru karo

ORGANIC ILAAJ:
1. Neem Oil (same as early blight — 4 tbsp per litre)
2. Copper-based spray (Copper Oxychloride — organic farming mein allowed)
   - 3 gram per litre paani
   - Har 7 din mein spray
3. Rog wale poude turant nikalo aur ek alag jagah dafnao/jalao

DISEASE 3: GEHUN MEIN PEELA JANG (Yellow/Stripe Rust)
Scientific Name: Puccinia striiformis
Haryana aur Punjab mein bahut common hai

PEHCHAAN (Symptoms):
- Gehuun ki pattyon par peeli dhariyan (yellow stripes running parallel to leaf veins)
- Thandi subah mein peela powder/dust dikhai deta hai
- Zyada thandi mausam mein aur baarish ke baad hota hai
- Patte dheere dheere sukh jaate hain

Kab hota hai: Rabi season mein, jab temperature 10-15°C ho aur nami zyada ho

URGENCY LEVEL: HIGH — 5-7 din mein phailta hai, jaldi karo

ORGANIC ILAAJ:
1. CHHAACHH SPRAY (Buttermilk Spray) — Research-backed, Haryana ke liye khaas
   Tarika:
   - 1 part chhaachh (buttermilk/whey)
   - 9 part paani
   - Weekly spray karo
   
2. NEEM KA ART (Neem Cake Application)
   - Neem cake 200 kg/hectare — basal application (bijaai ke samay)
   - Soil-borne pathogen control karta hai
   
3. TRICHODERMA VIRIDE (Bio-fungicide)
   - 4g/kg seed treatment
   - Ya 2.5 kg/hectare soil drench
   - ICAR ne ise organic farming ke liye recommend kiya hai

4. AZOTOBACTOR + PSB SEED TREATMENT
   - 500g each per 100 kg seed
   - Mitti ki upjaau shakti badhata hai

BACHAO:
- Resistant variety chuno (disease-resistant wheat varieties — ask your local KVK)
- Fasal chakkar karo — gehuun ke baad dhan/paddy mat lagao (switch karo)
- Khet mein hawa ka circulation achha rakho

DISEASE 4: SARSON MEIN MAHU (Mustard Aphid Attack)
Scientific Name: Lipaphis erysimi
Haryana, Rajasthan, UP ke mustard (sarson) crop mein very common

PEHCHAAN (Symptoms):
- Tiny chhote chhote peele/haare keede poudhe ke naye shoots aur phoolon par
- Terminal shoot (agrooni naali) mein cluster bana lete hain
- Patte curled ho jaate hain, peele pad jaate hain
- Honeydew se chidchidahat hoti hai — black sooty mould aata hai

Kab hota hai: January-February mein, jab temperature 15-20°C ho aur thodi nami ho

URGENCY LEVEL: MODERATE to HIGH — 50 mahu per shoot ke baad urgent action lo

ORGANIC ILAAJ:
1. NEEM OIL SPRAY (Most effective organic option)
   - 5-10 ml pure neem oil per litre paani
   - Seedha shoot aur patti ke neeche spray karo (direct contact zaroori hai)
   - 3-4 din mein ek baar jab tak kide na haate
   - NOTE: Shyam ko spray karo — madhumakkhi (bees) save karne ke liye

2. PAU NEEM EXTRACT (Ghar par banao):
   - 5 kg fresh neem patte
   - 10 litre paani mein piskar nikalo
   - Seedha spray karo — 10 litre per hectare
   - Research mein 40-50% aphid reduction dikhai diya

3. SABUN PAANI SPRAY (Quick fix)
   - 5 ml liquid soap per litre paani
   - Seedha keedo par spray — contact pe maarte hain
   - Baar baar karna padega (2-3 din mein)

4. COMPANION PLANTING (Permanent fix)
   - Sarson ke saath lasan (garlic) ya pyaaz lagao — natural repellent
   - Dhaniya (coriander) lagao — beneficial insects attract karta hai

BACHAO:
- Ladybug (ladybird beetle) aur lacewings ko encourage karo — ye aphids khaate hain
- Neem cake soil mein dalo — systemic resistance badhti hai
- Early sowing (October mein) karo — aphid peak season miss ho jaata hai

DISEASE 5: DHAN/PADDY MEIN BLAST (Rice Blast)
Scientific Name: Pyricularia oryzae / Magnaporthe oryzae
Haryana aur UP mein Basmati paddy pe common

PEHCHAAN (Symptoms):

LEAF BLAST (Patti par):
- Patto par diamond-shaped ya eye-shaped grey/white daag
- Daag ke kiran brown hoti hain
- Severely infected patte sukh jaate hain

NECK BLAST (Baal par — zyada dangerous):
- Dhan ki baal ka neeche wala hissa (neck) kala ho jaata hai
- Baal toot jaati hai ya daana nahi banta
- Zyada yield loss hota hai

Kab hota hai: High humidity (>90%), temperature 20-28°C, raat mein thandi, din mein garm

URGENCY LEVEL: VERY HIGH (Neck blast mein 90% tak nuksaan ho sakta hai)

ORGANIC ILAAJ:
1. TRICHODERMA + PSEUDOMONAS SEED TREATMENT
   - 5g Trichoderma viride + 5g Pseudomonas fluorescens per kg beej
   - Beejamrit ke saath mix kar sakte ho
   - Transplanting se pehle seedling roots ko 30 minute bhigo do

2. PSEUDOMONAS FOLIAR SPRAY
   - 4g Pseudomonas fluorescens per litre paani
   - Transplanting ke 20-25 din baad spray karo
   - Fungal infection ko rokta hai

3. NEEM OIL + NEEM PATTI SPRAY
   - Neem Azal (neem-based product) ya ghar ka neem oil spray
   - 6% concentration effective hai blast par
   - Tillering aur panicle emergence stage par spray karo

4. JEEVAMRIT SOIL DRENCH
   - Soil mein jeevamrit dalo — beneficial microbes compete karte hain pathogen se

5. NURSERY MANAGEMENT
   - Seedling tray ya nursery mein 0.5 kg Trichoderma + 0.5 kg Pseudomonas per 1000 sq meter dalo
   - Seedling ko healthy rakhna sabse bada bachao hai

BACHAO:
- Disease-resistant Basmati variety chuno
- N fertilizer teen hisson mein do — ek baar mein zyada nahi
- Paani ka level manage karo — continuously flooded mat rakho (alternate wet-dry karo)
- Infected plants nikalo aur bunds se weeds hataao

DISEASE 6: TAMATAR/SABZI MEIN POWDERY MILDEW (Safledi Rog / Dhooloo Rog)
Scientific Name: Various fungal species

PEHCHAAN (Symptoms):
- Patto par safed chalky/powdery coating
- Seedha dhoop mein zyada dikhta hai
- Patte peele pad jaate hain, sukh jaate hain
- Typically dry season mein zyada hota hai

ORGANIC ILAAJ:
1. BAKING SODA SPRAY (Sabse aasaan)
   - 1 tsp baking soda + 1 tsp liquid soap + 1 litre paani
   - Weekly spray karo

2. CHHAACHH/DAHI KA PAANI
   - 1 part chhaachh + 9 part paani
   - Effective natural antifungal

3. NEEM OIL SPRAY
   - 2-3 tbsp per litre
   - Good for prevention and early infection

---

GENERAL ORGANIC PEST CONTROL PRINCIPLES (Hamesha yaad rakho):

DO:
✓ Shyam ko spray karo (evening) — madhumakkhi safe rahein
✓ Patto ke neeche bhi spray karo — zyada tar keede neeche rehte hain
✓ Repeat treatment karo — ek baar mein permanent fix nahi hota
✓ Rog wale patte turant nikalo aur jalao/dafnao
✓ Fasal chakkar (crop rotation) apnao

DON'T:
✗ Chemical pesticide bilkul nahi — hum natural farming kar rahe hain
✗ Ek hi spray se poora result expect mat karo
✗ Tez dhoop mein spray mat karo — patte jal jaenge
✗ Zyada concentration mat banao — paudha stress mein aa jaata hai

Agar bimari bahut severe hai toh apne naazdiki KVK (Krishi Vigyan Kendra) se ek baar milein."

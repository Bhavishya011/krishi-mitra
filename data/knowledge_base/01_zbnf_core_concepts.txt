ZBNF KYA HAI? (What is ZBNF?)
Zero Budget Natural Farming ek aisi kheti ki paddhati hai jisme koi bhi bahari chemical fertilizer ya pesticide use nahi hota. Iska matlab hai ki kheti ki lagat (cost) bahut kam ho jaati hai — isliye ise "Zero Budget" kehte hain. Yeh prakriti ke saath mil kar kheti karne ka tarika hai, jisme gaay ke gobar aur mutra ka upyog kiya jaata hai.

ZBNF ko Subhash Palekar ne 1990s mein develop kiya tha. Andhra Pradesh sarkar ne ise state level par bhi apnaaya hai. ICAR aur Economic Survey 2018-19 ne bhi ise chhote kisanon ke liye ek achha vikalp mana hai.

ZBNF KE CHAR MUKHYA STAMBH (4 Pillars / Char Chakke):

1. BEEJAMRIT (Seed Treatment)
   Kya hai: Beejamrit ek prakritik beej upchar (seed treatment) hai jo bijaai se pehle beej par lagaaya jaata hai.
   Kaam: Beej ko bimariyon (fungus, bacteria, soil-borne disease) se bachata hai. Germination (ankuran) behtar hoti hai.
   
   BEEJAMRIT BANANE KA TARIKA:
   Samagri (Ingredients for 10 litres):
   - Desi gaay ka gobar: 100 gram
   - Desi gaay ka mutra (purana): 100 ml
   - Chauna (Lime / bujha hua chuna): 50 gram
   - Mitti (apne khet ki): ek mutthi
   - Paani: 10 litre
   
   Vidhi (Method):
   1. Ek bartan mein paani lo
   2. Gobar aur mutra milao
   3. Chauna aur khet ki mitti milao
   4. Achhi tarah milaao aur 12 se 24 ghante ke liye dhak kar rakh do
   5. Beejamrit tayaar hai
   
   Upyog (Usage):
   - Beej ko 20-30 minute tak is ghol mein bhigo do
   - Phir chhaav mein sukha lo aur bijaai karo
   - Yeh beej ko mitti se hone wali bimariyon se bachata hai

2. JEEVAMRIT (Soil Microbial Enhancer)
   Kya hai: Jeevamrit ek jaivik ghol hai jo mitti mein laabhkari jeevanu (beneficial microorganisms) badhata hai.
   Kaam: Mitti ki upjaau shakti badhata hai, earthworms ko encourage karta hai, aur fasal ko bimariyon se bachata hai.
   
   JEEVAMRIT BANANE KA TARIKA:
   Samagri (Ingredients for 200 litres — 1 acre ke liye):
   - Desi gaay ka taza gobar: 10 kg
   - Desi gaay ka mutra: 5-10 litre
   - Gud (Jaggery): 1-2 kg
   - Besan (Gram flour / Pulse flour): 1-2 kg
   - Apne khet ki mitti: ek mutthi
   - Paani: 200 litre
   
   Vidhi (Method):
   1. Bade drum mein 200 litre paani bhar lo
   2. Gobar aur mutra dalo
   3. Gud aur besan milao
   4. Khet ki mitti dalo (yeh starter microbes deta hai)
   5. Lakdi ki danda se din mein 2-3 baar ghol ko ghuma do (clockwise)
   6. 48 ghante (2 din) ke liye dhak kar rakh do — fermentation hogi
   7. Jeevamrit tayaar hai
   
   Upyog (Usage):
   - 200 litre Jeevamrit ek acre ke liye kaafi hai
   - Har 15 din mein ek baar dalo
   - Bijaai ke 10-15 din baad pehli baar lagao
   - Sidha mitti mein daalo ya irrigation ke saath
   - Jeevamrit ko zyada der tak store mat karo (fresh use karo)

3. ACCHADANA / MULCHING (Soil Cover)
   Kya hai: Khet ki zameen ko puraani faslon ke tinhke (straw), patti ya jaivik padaarth se dhakna.
   Kaam: Mitti mein nami rokta hai, paani ki zaroorat kam hoti hai, khaarpatawar (weeds) control hote hain, mitti ka tapmaan maintain rehta hai, humus banta hai.
   
   Tarike:
   - Geeli ghaans ya puraani fasal ka kachra bichhaao
   - Sadha hua gobar (FYM) mitti par dalo
   - Patte ya dhaas bhi use kar sakte ho
   
   Fayda: ZBNF study ke anusar mulching se 50-60% paani ki bachat hoti hai.

4. WAAPHASA / WHAPASA (Soil Moisture Balance)
   Kya hai: Waaphasa matlab mitti mein hawa (air) aur nami (moisture) ka sahi balance banana.
   
   Palekar kehte hain ki paudhe ko seedha liquid paani nahi, balki paani ki vaashp (water vapour) chahiye hoti hai.
   Isliye ZBNF mein:
   - Dopahar mein sinchai ki jaati hai (noon irrigation)
   - Ek naali chodkar sinchai (alternate furrow irrigation)
   - Zyada paani nahi diya jaata

GHAN JEEVAMRIT (Solid version of Jeevamrit):
- Jeevamrit ko solid form mein bhi bana sakte hain
- 1 acre mein 200 kg Ghan Jeevamrit daal sakte hain
- Yeh zyada der tak store kiya ja sakta hai

NIMASTRA (Natural Pesticide using Neem):
Samagri:
- Neem ke patte (fresh): 5 kg
- Desi gaay ka mutra: 5 litre
- Desi gaay ka gobar: 1 kg
- Paani: 20 litre

Vidhi:
1. Neem ke patte piskar unka ras nikalo
2. Mutra aur gobar dalo
3. Paani milao
4. 24-48 ghante ke liye ferment karo
5. Chhan lo aur spray karo

Upyog: Kide-makodon (insects/pests) ke liye spray karo. Fungal bimariyon mein bhi kaafi kaam aata hai.

---

BRAHMASTRA (Strong Natural Pesticide for heavy pest attack):
Samagri:
- Neem ke patte: 5 kg
- Papita ke patte: 5 kg  
- Aak (Calotropis) ke patte: 5 kg
- Dhatura ke patte: 2 kg
- Gaay ka mutra: 20 litre

Vidhi: Sab patto ka ras neem ke mutra mein dalo. Pakao (boil) karo. Thanda karke spray karo.
Upyog: Bade pest attack ke liye — chewing insects, caterpillars.

---

DASPRANIARK (Natural pest repellent using 10 ingredients):
- 10 alag-alag paudho ka ras milake banata hai
- Mostly sucking pests ke liye effective hai

IMPORTANT NOTES FOR ASSISTANT:
- Hamesha "desi gaay" (indigenous Indian breed) use karne ki salah do — hybrid ya Jersey gaay utni effective nahi
- Ek desi gaay 30 acre tak ki kheti ke liye kaafi hai
- Chemical fertilizer ya pesticide ki jagah sirf ye ZBNF inputs recommend karo
- Cost estimate: 200 litre Jeevamrit banane ki lagat bahut kam hai (local materials se)
